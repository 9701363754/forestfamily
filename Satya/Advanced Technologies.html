Advanced Technology

The Latest in Dental Technology

By investing in the latest dental technology, our dental practice is able to provide dental treatments that are faster, more comfortable and safer than ever before. Our use of modern dental technology throughout our services is a highlighted advantage for patients of all ages.

Digital X-Rays: Ensuring the lowest radiation dosage possible, the doctor captures high-quality images in 3D volume using the Sirona Galileos Comfort Plus. Through a 14 second high-definition scan, diagnosis can be obtained quickly and comfortably. Click here to learn more about the best diagnostic tool on the market.

The Jaw Dropper: The Jaw Dropper stretches and relaxes the jaw muscles, making it easy for our patients to keep their mouth open comfortably. The Jaw Dropper also allows for hands-free fluid evacuation, which patients love.

ZOOM! Whitening: Lighten your teeth by up to eight shades in just one hour with our in-office Phillips ZOOM! Whitening system. It is one of the most comfortable and convenient way to enhance your smile.

Cone Beam: Cone beam 3-D imaging provides profound benefits for both patient and dentist. It comfortably captures all angles of the face, teeth and jaw and allows the doctor to provide more accurate procedures such as dental implants.

Click here to learn more

Paperless Practice Management: By keeping your dental history and imaging in our electronic database, your personal records are easily retrievable and better secured.

To learn more about our advanced dental technology, please contact Arka Family Dental today. We pursue excellence when it comes to caring for your smile.
